Privacy Policy

App: Misheard Phrases
Developer: BluePeak Studios Ltd
Last updated: 9 December 2025

BluePeak Studios Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Misheard Phrases mobile app handles information.

1. Information we collect

The Misheard Phrases app is designed to be played without creating an account or providing personal details. The app itself does not collect or store any information that directly identifies you as an individual.

The app may store limited, non-personal data locally on your device, such as:

  • Game progress (e.g. completed levels, scores, or coins).
  • Basic settings or preferences (e.g. sound on/off, selected category).

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Analytics and third-party services

At this time, the Misheard Phrases app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

If we introduce analytics or additional services in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, request your consent.

3. In-app purchases

If the app offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, these are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Any billing information you provide is handled directly by Apple in accordance with their terms and privacy policy.

4. Children’s privacy

Misheard Phrases is intended for a general audience and does not require children to submit personal information to use the app. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5. Your choices

Because the app does not collect personal information or use cloud accounts, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf.

If you wish to clear game progress or settings, you can usually do so by:

  • Deleting and reinstalling the app; or
  • Using any “reset progress” option within the app (if available).

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features or integrate third-party services. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
