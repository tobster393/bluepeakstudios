Misheard Phrases ‚Äì app overview

A say-what-you-see style party game for nights in, pre-drinks, and group calls.

What it is

Misheard Phrases is a misheard-phrase guessing game that lives on your phone but feels like a card game. Each card shows a phrase that looks like nonsense, but when you sound it out, it becomes a real word, phrase, movie, song or saying.

How it plays
  • Pick a category and difficulty.
  • Read the scrambled phrase on screen out loud.
  • Listen for the real phrase hidden in the sounds.
  • Shout it out when it clicks ‚Äì then move to the next one.
Key features
  • Multiple modes: solo, timed, and friends/party modes.
  • Different categories like common phrases, movies, songs, and more.
  • Built to run on a single device ‚Äì no sign-ups, no accounts.
  • Designed for quick rounds that fit into real life.
